Pan-Roasted Salmon With Arugula and Avocado Salad

Ingredients

4 (5- to 6-ounce) salmon filets, preferably wild
Kosher salt and freshly ground black pepper
1/3 cup plus 3 tablespoons extra-virgin olive oil, divided
4 cups baby arugula
2 tablespoons fresh juice from 1 lemon
Grated Parmigiano-Reggiano, for serving
1 ripe Hass avocado, pitted and cut into 1/2-inch cubes
1 tablespoon very finely chopped parsley

Instructions

1. Press salmon filets between paper towels to dry surfaces thoroughly. Season on all sides with salt and pepper.
2. Heat 1/3 cup oil in a large stainless steel skillet over medium-high heat until shimmering.
3. Add salmon filets skin side-down. Reduce heat to medium-low.
4. Cook until skin is rendered and crisp, about 6 minutes.
5. In a large bowl, combine arugula, lemon juice, 3 tablespoons olive oil, and avocado. Season with salt and pepper and add grated cheese.
6. Flip salmon and cook on second side until a thermometer inserted into the thickest part registers 120F for medium rare or 130F for medium, about 1 minute longer.
7. Transfer salmon to a paper towel-lined plate. Spoon salad onto plates, top with salmon, and serve.

Time

10 Minutes.
