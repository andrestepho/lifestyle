Zucchini Noodles

Ingredients

2 large zucchinis
2 cups of fresh basil
1/4 cup of raw cashews
2-3 garlic cloves
1/4 cup + 2 tablespoons nutritional yeast
1/4 cup + 3 tablespoons quality olive oil
2 tablespoons lemon juice
1/3 teaspoon sea salt
1/2 teaspoon black pepper
pinch of nutmeg

Instructions

1. Use a food processor to process garlic cloves, 3 tablespoons olive oil, pepper, and cashews. Do until smooth.
2. Add nutritional yeast and salt and process.
3. Add basil and 1/4 cup olive oil and process.
4. Add lemon juice and process.
5. Chop about 1/2" off both ends of the zucchini and spiralize.
6. Add zuccchini noodles to a large bowl and mix in with pesto.
7. Serve and enjoy!

Time

10 Minutes.
